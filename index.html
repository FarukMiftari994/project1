Vitamin Well Drinks

Vitamin Well is a Swedish company and also a brand for vitamin drinks.
The company was founded in 2006 by Jonas Pettersson and the drinks were released on the market in May 2008.
The ambition then was to create a vitamin drink without sugar.
The company also owns the brands Nocco, Barebells and Nobe Aloe Vera.
